TalkReserve

This app will create an efficient process to serve a large scale of customers of the New Life Resource Cafe and the 2000+ members of New Life Christain Fellowship Church. The app holds an archive of recorded sermons preached from 2008 to 2014, total of 1,165. The Cafe customers will be able to quickly place a audio sermon reservation at the customer service desk. In addition members of New Life Christian Fellowship church will be able to sign up for open volunteer positions within various ministries in the church.

Instructions on how to use this application

1st. Search for the product the customer is requesting. You may search by date or topic. Select the topic.
2nd. Take the information from the customer to fill out the information, via name, phone, number, and etc.
3rd. If needed please add any additional information in the notes section.
4th. After topic is selected and the form is complete click "SEND".
5th. When finish with order click the delete button to remove the information from the box.
This will send the product order to the Reserve@outlook.com
